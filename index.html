Match Format2 Sets and 10 Point Tie Breaker
Playoff FormatWe follow IPL (Indian Premier Leauge) Format on the playoffs
Playoff Rules Part 14 Teams with most points on July 7th will advance to Playoffs
Playoff Rules Part 2If more than one team has the same point at the end of the Round robin games, the tie will be broken by the first of the following procedures that does so. Head-to-Head Competition - The competitor who won the match between the teams is declared the winner. Number of Sets – team that lost the fewest number of sets. Number of Games - team that lost the fewest number of games. Tie breaker game – If none of the above breaks a tie, the two players must play one tie breaker game. Players must have a toss to decide the service and the side.
